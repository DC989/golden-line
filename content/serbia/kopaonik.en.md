## About the Destination

Kopaonik, known as the "Silver Mountain," is Serbia's largest ski resort with over 55 km of alpine slopes and 12 km of cross-country skiing trails. In winter, it offers excellent conditions for skiers and snowboarders, while in summer it's ideal for hiking, walking and enjoying clear mountain air. Kopaonik National Park is home to many protected plant and animal species.
