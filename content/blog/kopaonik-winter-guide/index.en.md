## About the Resort

Kopaonik sits on the mountain of the same name in central Serbia, at an altitude of approximately 1,770 metres. The resort offers:

- **55+ km of ski runs** for all skill levels
- **24 lifts and chairlifts**
- Season runs from **December to April**
- Night skiing on selected slopes
