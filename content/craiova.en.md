## About the Destination: Advent 2025

The Craiova Christmas Market is regularly ranked among the **most beautiful in Europe** (by _European Best Destinations_). What awaits you:

- **Millions of Lights:** The city is adorned with unique light installations that feature a different theme every year (e.g., The Snow Queen, Alice in Wonderland).
- **Ice Rink & Concerts:** Entertainment for the whole family in Mihai Viteazul Square.
- **Traditional Chalets:** Handmade souvenirs, mulled wine, Romanian specialties, and holiday treats.
